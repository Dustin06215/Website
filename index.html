Subject line: Why you aren't productive (or not as productive as possible)


There are mostly only some subtle things to do to change your productivity in good or bad.

The smallest changes have a huge impact on your work and if you change the right thing,

then you won’t have any problems with focus or distractions.

First of all, you need to get rid of all distractions:

-> Put your phone on silent and out of sight, you can even lay it in a different room till your work is done.

-> Have a clean work desk, no unnecessary things on it, only equipment needed for your work or task.

-> If you work on a pc or a laptop, close all tabs you don’t need and NO music, while working

Secondly, you need to gain full focus:

-> Make a schedule and a timeframe for your tasks to get structure and to be hyper-concentrated on   only one thing at a time

-> Have small breaks of 5-10 minutes after bigger tasks or after multiple smaller tasks, aim to have a brake after around every 2-3 hours

-> Don’t try to do multiple tasks at once or interrupt your current work with a different task, this is a big focus killer, because you step back from your work and tap into another
